# How to Publish in Q1 Journals and Top-Tier Conferences

A practical three-day workshop on research, academic writing, experimental design, and publication.

## Topics

- Defining a research problem
- Writing research questions, aims, and objectives
- Conducting a literature review
- Reproducing and improving baseline methods
- Designing strong experiments
- Writing an academic paper
- Selecting suitable journals and conferences
- Responding to reviewer comments
